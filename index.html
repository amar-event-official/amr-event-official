Thank you for your interest in our MVP!

Our Minimum Viable Product (MVP) offers the core features of our event management platform, designed to simplify the event creation and management process. With this MVP, organizers can:

  • Create and manage events with key details like name, date, and location.
  • Allow attendees to register and purchase tickets online with ease.
  • Access an event-specific website to manage all related activities.
  • Get real-time notifications about updates and changes to events.

By launching the MVP, we aim to gather valuable feedback to continuously improve and enhance the platform to better meet the needs of event organizers and attendees.

Expected release date: 01 March 2025
